About me

I am Sushank, a graduate in Forestry with honors in 2023. My hobbies include reading books and novels, watching movies and TV series, writing novels and short stories, hiking, and traveling.

Recently, I developed a strong enthusiasm for academic research and have been dedicatedly focusing on it. I have published a research paper on vegetation analysis, and two additional manuscripts are currently under review in Q3 journals.

I am leading research on habitat suitability modeling of bamboo species, deforestation modeling, and land cover and land use change detection in protected areas of Nepal. I have also participated in multiple other studies.

Working on these projects, I have developed skills in RStudio, ArcMap, QGIS, Bayesian Modeling, Python, and Javascript.
