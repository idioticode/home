Species Distribution Modeling of Bamboo

I am leading a research project to determine the current and future (2050, 2070, and 2090) suitable habitats for seven bamboo species in Nepal. Our study utilizes nineteen bioclimatic variables and three topographical variables downloaded from Worldclim, along with soil data from FAO/UNESCO.

Initially, I performed habitat modeling using the MaxEnt model with the 'dismo' package in R. Recognizing that ensemble modeling is better than a single model, I utilized the 'sdm' package for the final model building and habitat prediction. This approach integrates various models (e.g., Random Forest, MaxEnt, Support Vector Machine), to enhance accuracy and reliability.

The modeling phase is now complete, and we are in the process of developing the manuscript.
